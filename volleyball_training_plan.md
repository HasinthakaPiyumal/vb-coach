# 🏐 5-Day Volleyball Training Plan — All Skills, Every Day

> **Athlete Profile:** Beginner · Poor attack power · Poor jumping · Poor blocking · Reduced court vision (glasses off)
> **Equipment:** Volleyball, wall, outdoor space, volleyball court access
> **Total Session:** ~2 hours 10 minutes per day

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Daily Structure:**
> | Phase | Location | Duration |
> |---|---|---|
> | **Phase 1** — Warm-Up, Strength, Balance, Wall Drills | Indoor / Wall / Small space | **90 min** |
> | **Phase 2** — Court Training | Volleyball court | **40 min** |
>
> Take **2 rest days** after the 5-day cycle (e.g., Mon–Fri train, Sat–Sun rest).

> [!TIP]
> **3 Levels for Every Drill.** Start at **Level 1**. When you can complete it comfortably for a full week, move up. Don't rush — clean technique at Level 1 beats sloppy technique at Level 3.

---

## 🗓️ Weekly Overview

| Day | Strength Theme | Phase 1 (90 min) | Phase 2 — Court (40 min) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Lower Body Power | Warm-up + Strength + Wall skills | Court serve, attack, block, game sim |
| **2** | Upper Body & Core | Warm-up + Strength + Wall skills | Court serve, attack, block, game sim |
| **3** | Agility & Explosiveness | Warm-up + Strength + Wall skills | Court serve, attack, block, game sim |
| **4** | Full Body Circuit | Warm-up + Strength + Wall skills | Court serve, attack, block, game sim |
| **5** | Game-Speed Conditioning | Warm-up + Strength + Wall skills | Court serve, attack, block, game sim |
| **6–7** | **REST** | Light walking or stretching only | — |

# Day 1 — Lower Body Power

---

## PHASE 1 — Indoor / Wall / Small Space (90 min)

---

### 🔥 Warm-Up (12 min)

| Exercise | Duration / Reps |
|---|---|
| Light jog in place | 2 min |
| High knees | 30 sec × 2 |
| Butt kicks | 30 sec × 2 |
| Leg swings (front-to-back, each leg) | 15 each |
| Leg swings (side-to-side, each leg) | 15 each |
| Ankle circles | 15 each direction per foot |
| Bodyweight squats (slow, full range) | 10 reps |
| Hip circles | 15 each direction |
| Walking lunges (dynamic) | 10 each leg |

### 💪 Strength — Lower Body Power (30 min)

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bodyweight squats (ATG) | 4 × 15 | 45s | Thighs below parallel. Chest up, weight in heels. |
| Jump squats | 4 × 10 | 60s | Explode up, land softly on balls of feet. Maximum height. |
| Forward lunges | 3 × 12 each leg | 45s | Front knee behind toes. Drive through the heel. |
| Reverse lunges | 3 × 12 each leg | 45s | Step backward. Easier on knees, different muscle emphasis. |
| Calf raises (on curb/step) | 4 × 25 | 30s | 3-sec slow lowering. Critical for vertical jump. |
| Wall sit | 3 × 45 sec | 45s | Thighs parallel. Mental toughness builder. |
| Single-leg glute bridges | 3 × 15 each | 45s | Squeeze glute at top. Hold 2 sec. |
| Squat hold pulses | 3 × 20 | 45s | Hold at bottom of squat, pulse up/down 3 inches. Burns = working. |

### ⚖️ Balance & Stability (8 min)

| Exercise | Duration |
|---|---|
| Single-leg stand (eyes open) | 45 sec each leg |
| Single-leg stand (eyes closed) | 30 sec each leg — *critical for vision compensation* |
| Single-leg mini squats | 10 each leg |
| Single-leg deadlift (hold volleyball) | 8 each leg |
| Tandem stance walk (heel-to-toe, eyes closed) | 10 steps forward + 10 back |

### 🏐 Wall Skills Block (35 min)

#### Passing — Wall (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Wall forearm passing | 5 min | 2–3 m from wall. Consistent platform, arms straight, contact on forearms. Count consecutive — **goal per level below.** |
| Wall overhead passing | 5 min | Same distance. Fingertip contact, triangle hand shape above forehead. |

#### Setting — Wall (8 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Wall sets to target | 4 min | Set to a consistent spot (imagine 50cm box). Hands above forehead, fingertips only. |
| Self-sets (straight up) | 4 min | Set ball straight up, stay in same spot. Builds touch and soft hands. |

#### Attacking — Wall (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Standing arm swing (no ball) | 3 min | Bow-and-arrow → full overhead swing → wrist snap. 60+ reps. Elbow HIGH. |
| Wall hitting (standing) | 4 min | Toss up, hit into wall. Contact at highest point. Focus on wrist snap. |
| Approach footwork (no ball) | 3 min | L-R-L (right-handed) or R-L-R (left-handed). Last two steps quick "step-close." 25+ approaches. |

#### Vision Training (7 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Ball tracking (track & point) | 2.5 min | Toss at wall at varying angles/speeds. Follow with eyes, point at the ball. Don't catch. |
| Peripheral catch | 2.5 min | Fix eyes on wall point. Toss ball to edge of vision. Catch without turning head. |
| Sound tracking (eyes closed) | 2 min | Eyes closed. Bounce ball, clap when it hits the ground. Then bounce off wall and catch blind. |

### 🧘 Cool-Down & Transition (5 min)

- Standing quad stretch — 30 sec each leg
- Standing hamstring stretch — 30 sec each
- Calf stretch against wall — 30 sec each
- Quick forearm/wrist stretch — 30 sec
- Deep breathing — 1 min
- *Hydrate and head to the court* 🏃

## PHASE 2 — Volleyball Court (40 min)

---

#### Serving (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Underhand serve | 4 min | From behind end line. Focus on consistency — clear the net, land in the court. Track your score out of 10. |
| Overhand serve | 6 min | Start from attack line (3m), work backward as you improve. Toss in front of hitting shoulder, contact at highest point with open hand. Score out of 10. |

#### Attacking at the Net (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Approach + standing hit over net | 4 min | Self-toss near the net. Approach steps, hit the ball over. No jump yet at Level 1. Focus on swing timing with footwork. |
| Approach + jump hit over net | 6 min | Full sequence: toss near net → approach → jump → hit. Contact at peak of jump. Aim for the opposite court. Don't worry about power — timing is king. |

#### Blocking at the Net (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Block jumps at the net | 4 min | Face the net in ready position. Jump straight up, press hands over the net with fingers spread, wrists locked. Penetrate over the net — don't just reach up, push hands FORWARD over it. 20+ reps. |
| Slide-and-block along the net | 3 min | Start at center. Shuffle 2 steps left → block jump → shuffle back → 2 steps right → block jump. 12+ total block jumps. |
| Consecutive block jumps | 3 min | Jump, land, jump again immediately. Sets of 5 consecutive jumps. Rest 15 sec between sets. 4+ sets. Builds blocking endurance. |

#### Game Simulation (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Serve → sprint → pass | 4 min | Serve over the net, sprint to the other side, try to pass the ball before the 2nd bounce. Builds conditioning, urgency, and ball reading. |
| Full attack sequence | 3 min | Self-toss high near the net → approach → jump → hit → immediately transition to defensive position (low, ready). Repeat. Simulates offense-to-defense transition. |
| Visualization cool-down | 3 min | Sit courtside, close eyes. Visualize a full rally: serve receive → pass → set → approach → hit → score. Then visualize reading a hitter and blocking. See the ball clearly, hear the court sounds. |

## PHASE 2 — Volleyball Court (40 min)

---

#### Serving (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Overhand serve — consistency | 5 min | From your current distance (attack line → work back). 20+ serves. Track score out of each 10. |
| Overhand serve — placement | 5 min | Aim for specific court zones. Place a target (towel/shirt) in zone 1, 5, or 6. Score hits near the target. |

#### Attacking at the Net (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Approach + hit (focus: arm swing power) | 5 min | Self-toss, approach, hit over net. Today's focus: generate maximum wrist snap and arm speed. |
| Approach + directed hits | 5 min | Hit cross-court, then line, then short tip. Developing shot variety — not just power, but placement. |

#### Blocking at the Net (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Block jump + footwork combos | 5 min | Shuffle right → block → shuffle center → shuffle left → block. Continuous movement along net with quality block jumps. |
| Toss-and-block | 5 min | Toss the ball just above the net from one side, sprint to the other side and try to block/touch it before it lands. Trains timing and reaching over the net. |

#### Game Simulation (10 min)

| Drill | Time | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Serve → transition to defense | 4 min | Serve, then immediately drop into defensive ready position at a specific court position. Practice the serve → defensive transition. |
| Pass → set → hit sequence (solo) | 3 min | Toss ball up as if receiving a serve, pass to yourself, set to yourself near the net, then hit over. Full offensive sequence. |
| Mental rehearsal | 3 min | Eyes closed courtside. Visualize yourself executing everything you trained today — perfectly. See, hear, feel the court. |

# 👁️ Vision Compensation — Daily Habits

| Habit | Why It Helps |
|---|---|
| **Track the ball early** | Watch from the server's/hitter's hand. Earlier pickup = less reliance on sharp vision. |
| **Use your ears** | Hard hit vs. tip sounds different. Audio gives info before you see the ball clearly. |
| **Read body language** | Watch hitter's shoulder & arm angle — their body tells you where the ball goes. |
| **Communicate loudly** | Call "Mine!" / "Out!" / "Free!" early. Forces faster decisions. |
| **Return to base position** | Good positioning reduces last-second reactions. Always reset after each play. |
| **Consider sports goggles** | Strapped sports goggles rated for volleyball can be a game-changer. |
